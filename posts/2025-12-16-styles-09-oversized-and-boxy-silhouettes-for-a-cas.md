# Oversized and boxy silhouettes for a casual yet trendy look — 2025-12-16

Hey, fashionistas! Let's talk about how oversized and boxy silhouettes are totally in right now for achieving that effortlessly cool and comfy vibe. Forget about skin-tight everything, it's all about embracing the roomy and relaxed fit.
