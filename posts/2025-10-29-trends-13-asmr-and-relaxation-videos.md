# ASMR and relaxation videos — 2025-10-29

Hey there, my fellow teens! Let’s chat about ASMR and those ultra-relaxing videos that are taking over the internet. Have you ever experienced that tingling sensation on the back of your neck while watching someone whisper into a microphone or crinkle paper? Whether you’re into gentle tapping, satisfying soap-cutting, or peaceful nature sounds, there’s an ASMR video out there for everyone. These videos are like a virtual spa day for your brain, helping you unwind and de-stress after a long day of school or drama with friends.
