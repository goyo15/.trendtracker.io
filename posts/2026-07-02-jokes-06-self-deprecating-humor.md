# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-07-02

Hey squad, let’s talk about self-deprecating humor! 🙌🏼 Sure, it can be funny to poke fun at yourself once in a while, but remember, there’s a fine line between self-deprecating humor and straight-up self-bashing. 🚫 It’s all fun and games until it starts to chip away at your self-esteem. So, pause before you make that self-deprecating joke and ask yourself, “Am I truly okay with this?” Because babe, you’re awesome and you deserve to treat yourself with kindness and respect. 💖 Don’t let the laughs come at the cost of your own self-worth. Remember, you’re a queen/king and should never settle for anything less. 👑 Let’s keep the humor light and the self-love heavy, because you’re worth it! Let’s lift each other up, not bring ourselves down. Keep slayin’! #SelfLove #PositiveVibesOnly 🌟✨
