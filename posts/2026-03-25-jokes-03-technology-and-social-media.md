# Technology and Social Media — 2026-03-25

Hey there, tech-savvy squad! Let’s talk about the ever-evolving world of technology and social media. From TikTok trends to Instagram stories, we’re constantly bombarded with digital distractions. But hold up, is all this screen time affecting our real-life connections?
