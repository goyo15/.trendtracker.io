# 90s Grunge – Flannel shirts, combat boots, and a rebellious attitude — 2026-05-16

Hey there, fellow rebels! Let's take a trip down memory lane to the iconic era of 90s grunge fashion. Picture this: flannel shirts tied around the waist, combat boots stomping all over conformity, and an attitude that screamed "I make my own rules."

Grunge wasn't just a fashion statement, it was a whole vibe. It was about channeling your inner rocker, embracing the messy hair and don't-care attitude. Grunge icons like Kurt Cobain and Courtney Love inspired a generation to express themselves through ripped jeans, band tees, and layers upon layers of flannel.

So throw on your favorite flannel, lace up those combat boots, and let your rebellious spirit shine. The 90s grunge scene may be a blast from the past, but its influence is still alive and kicking in today's fashion world. Stay edgy, stay cool, and most importantly, stay true to yourself. Peace out! ✌️ #GrungeLife #ThrowbackFashion
