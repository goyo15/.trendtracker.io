# Plant parenting and indoor gardening tips and hacks — 2026-04-07

Hey plant parents! 🌿 Looking to up your indoor gardening game? Let’s talk tips and hacks!

Tip 1: Light is everything! Make sure your plant babies are getting the right amount of sunlight. Research your plants’ light requirements and place them accordingly.

Tip 2: Don’t overwater! Too much water can lead to root rot. Check the soil moisture before watering again. Your plants will thank you!

Hack 1: Use a humidity tray to create a moist environment for your plant’s leaves. Simply place a tray with water and pebbles under your plant’s pot.

Hack 2: Make your own plant fertilizer using coffee grounds! Mix used coffee grounds into your plant’s soil for a natural nutrient boost.

So, there you have it! With these tips and hacks, your indoor garden will be thriving in no time. Happy planting! 🌱 #PlantParenting #IndoorGardeningTips
