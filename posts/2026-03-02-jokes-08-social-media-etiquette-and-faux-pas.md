# Social media etiquette and faux pas — 2026-03-02

Hey all you trendsetters and vibe-chasers! Let’s chat about social media etiquette because let’s face it, we’ve all witnessed some major faux pas online. So, rule number one: don’t be a keyboard warrior. It’s cool to have opinions, but respect others’ views too — no need to start a virtual brawl. And please, for the love of selfies, stop with the excessive hashtags. We get it, you’re crushing it, but too many hashtags is a major turn off. Oh, and can we finally address the issue of oversharing? We don’t need a play-by-play of your breakfast, Karen. Keep it real, but keep some mystery too. Lastly, let’s not forget to support each other. Double-tap that friend’s post, leave a nice comment, spread positivity! Let’s make the digital world a rad place to hang out. Peace out, social media gurus! #StayClassy #NoFilter
