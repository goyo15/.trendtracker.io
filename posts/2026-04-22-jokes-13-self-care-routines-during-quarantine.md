# Self-care routines during quarantine — 2026-04-22

Hey there, quarantine queens and kings! In the wild rollercoaster ride that is 2020, taking care of ourselves has never been more crucial. With so much chaos going on around us, it's important to carve out a little slice of sanity with some bomb self-care routines.

First off, prioritize sleep like it's your job. Getting those Z's is essential for keeping your mind sharp and your mood in check. Plus, who doesn't love waking up feeling refreshed and ready to slay the day?

Next up, don't forget to move that booty! Whether it's a quick workout, a dance party in your room, or a relaxing yoga session, keeping your bod active is key to staying strong and stress-free.

And lastly, show yourself some love with a little pampering sesh. Face masks, bubble baths, or just binge-watching your fave show – do whatever makes your soul happy.
