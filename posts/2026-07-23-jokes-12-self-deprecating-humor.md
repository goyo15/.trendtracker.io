# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-07-23

Hey, hey, fellow teens! Let’s talk about self-deprecating humor – you know, that thing we all do when we crack jokes at our own expense. It’s like a defense mechanism for awkwardness, insecurity, or just wanting to lighten the mood. 

But here’s the dealio, babes – while a little self-roasting can be funny, too much can be a major bummer. It’s all fun and games until you start believing the jokes you’re telling about yourself.

So, let’s keep it real and find that balance, okay? Embrace your quirks and imperfections, but also celebrate your awesomeness! There’s nothing wrong with a good laugh at your own expense, but remember that you’re fabulous just the way you are.

So, keep the humor coming, but don’t forget to show yourself some love too. Stay sassy, stay fierce, and keep slaying, my darlings! Peace out! ✌️ #SelfLoveIsTheBestLove
