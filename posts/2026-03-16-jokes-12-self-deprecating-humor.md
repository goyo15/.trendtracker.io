# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-03-16

Hey fam, let's talk about self-deprecating humor – the fine line between hilarious and harmful. Look, poking fun at yourself can be a mood booster and show you don't take life too seriously. But when it becomes a cover-up for real insecurities or negative self-talk, it's time to hit pause. Your worth isn't a punchline, so don't let self-deprecation turn into self-deprecation. Remember, you're a boss in your own right and deserve respect, even from yourself. Next time you wanna crack a joke at your own expense, ask yourself: is this coming from a place of self-love or self-doubt?

Just a reminder from one teen to another – keep the humor light, but keep that self-worth heavy, fam! 💪#LoveYourselfFirst #HumorResponsibly
