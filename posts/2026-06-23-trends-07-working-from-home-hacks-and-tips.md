# "Working From Home" hacks and tips — 2026-06-23

Hey guys, welcome back to the blog! Today I'm spilling the tea on all the best "Working From Home" hacks and tips. Let's face it, being productive in your pajamas can be a challenge, but fear not, I've got you covered.

First up, create a designated workspace to get in the zone. Whether it's a cozy corner or a DIY desk setup, having a dedicated area for work can do wonders for your focus. Next, set a schedule and stick to it. Treat your work day like you would in an office setting to avoid distractions (looking at you, Netflix).

Don't forget to take breaks! Get up, stretch, grab a snack, or even do a mini dance party to keep your energy up. And lastly, communicate with your team often to stay connected and on track.

So there you have it, my top WFH hacks and tips. Stay productive, stay comfy, and slay that work-from-home game! Peace out.
