# Self-Deprecating Humor — 2026-02-25

Hey, hey, hey! Let's talk about self-deprecating humor, shall we? 🙈 Sure, it can be hilarious to crack a joke about yourself, but where do we draw the line between funny and straight-up self-dragging? 🤔
