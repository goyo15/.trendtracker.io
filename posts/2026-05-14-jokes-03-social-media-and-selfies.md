# Social Media and Selfies — 2026-05-14

Hey, fellow teens! Let’s talk about social media and selfies. I mean, who doesn’t love a good selfie, am I right? But here’s the thing: social media can sometimes mess with our self-esteem. We see all these perfect pics of people having the time of their lives, and it’s easy to start comparing ourselves. 

But listen up, babes, don’t fall into that trap! Remember, social media is just a highlight reel. People only post their best moments, not the messy, real stuff. So next time you’re scrolling through your feed, take it with a grain of salt. 

And don’t forget, selfies are supposed to be fun! Embrace your unique beauty and snap away, but don’t let the number of likes define your worth. You’re fabulous just the way you are, so keep slaying those selfies, and remember to keep it real both online and off. 💁📸 #SelfieQueen #LoveYourself
