# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-05-22

Hey, fellow teens! Let’s chat about self-deprecating humor. It’s like a defense mechanism mixed with a sprinkle of sarcasm, am I right? We’re all guilty of poking fun at ourselves to get a laugh, but where do we draw the line between funny and just plain sad?

Sure, self-deprecation can be a great way to bond with friends and show that you don’t take yourself too seriously. But constantly putting yourself down can actually harm your self-esteem in the long run. Remember, it’s important to love yourself and treat yourself with kindness.

So, next time you’re about to crack a self-deprecating joke, ask yourself – is this coming from a place of confidence or insecurity? Let’s keep the humor light and the self-love strong. You’re awesome, and don’t ever forget it! Let’s keep the laughs coming, but always remember to lift ourselves up, not tear ourselves down.
