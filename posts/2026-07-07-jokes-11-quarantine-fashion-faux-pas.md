# Quarantine fashion faux pas — 2026-07-07

Hey, fashionistas! Let’s talk about the biggest quarantine fashion faux pas we’ve been seeing lately. First up, the “pyjamas all day” trend. I get it, being comfy is key, but let’s at least change out of those old, ratty PJs for our Zoom classes, yeah? Next, the infamous “mullet outfit” – business up top, party down below. We all know you’re wearing slippers with that business shirt, no need to pretend otherwise! And finally, the “sweatpants overload” – I get it, sweatpants are life, but maybe mix it up with some cute leggings or joggers once in a while? Remember, just because we’re in quarantine doesn’t mean we have to sacrifice style. Let’s keep it real, keep it cute, and most importantly, keep it comfy!
