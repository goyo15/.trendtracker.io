# Y2K fashion revival — 2026-01-18

Hey, fashionistas! Can we talk about the Y2K fashion revival that’s taking over our feeds right now? From butterfly clips to low-rise jeans, this trend is bringing back all the iconic looks from the early 2000s and I am so here for it.
