# Mental health check-ins and self-care routines — 2026-03-28

Hey there, fellow teens! Let’s talk mental health and self-care, something we can all use a little more of in our busy lives. It’s easy to get caught up in the stress of school, social drama, and everything in between, but taking care of our mental well-being is crucial.

First things first, mental health check-ins are a must. Take a moment to ask yourself how you’re really feeling. It’s okay not to be okay, but it’s important to acknowledge your emotions and reach out for support when needed.

Now, let’s chat about self-care routines. Whether it’s blasting your favorite music, going for a walk, doing some yoga, or just having a Netflix binge session, find what works for you and make it a regular part of your routine. Remember, self-care isn’t selfish, it’s necessary for a healthy mind and body.

So, let’s prioritize our mental health, practice self-care, and remember that it’s okay to give ourselves some love and attention. You’ve got this, take care of yourself, and keep shining bright!✨ #SelfLove #MentalHealthMatters
