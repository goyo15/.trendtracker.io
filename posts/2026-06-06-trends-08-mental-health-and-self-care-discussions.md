# Mental health and self-care discussions — 2026-06-06

Hey, fellow teens! Let's talk mental health and self-care, because it's crucial and trendy to take care of ourselves. 🌟

How many times have you heard, "Just relax," when you're stressed to the max? As if it's that easy! We need real talk about mental health, not just surface-level advice. It's okay to not be okay. It's okay to seek help. Therapy isn't just for "crazy" people – it's for anyone who wants to feel better.

Self-care isn't just face masks and bubble baths; it's setting boundaries, saying no when you need to, and prioritizing your mental well-being. Take a break from social media, get some fresh air, or call a friend. We're young, but we're not invincible. Let's normalize discussing mental health and self-care, because at the end of the day, your well-being is what truly matters. 💖 #SelfLoveRevolution
