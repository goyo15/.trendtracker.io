# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-06-13

Hey, hey, hey, my fellow teens! Let’s talk about self-deprecating humor - you know, the kind of jokes we make about ourselves that have everyone laughing, but secretly might reflect some deeper insecurities. It’s like a coping mechanism wrapped in sarcasm and served with a side of wit. 

Sure, it can be hilarious to roast yourself and poke fun at your own quirks, but where do we draw the line between self-deprecating humor and actually believing the negative things we say about ourselves? It’s all fun and games until we start internalizing those jokes and letting them affect our self-esteem.

So, let’s remember to balance the self-roasting with self-love, okay? It’s cool to laugh at yourself, but it’s even cooler to embrace your flaws and imperfections without the need for a punchline. Stay snarky, stay sassy, but most importantly, stay true to yourself! ✌️ #LoveYourself #ButAlsoLaughAtYourself
