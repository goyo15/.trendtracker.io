# Preppy fashion — 2026-06-29

Hey babes and dudes! Let’s talk about preppy fashion, because it’s totally making a comeback and I am here for it. Think pastels, collared shirts, cozy sweaters, plaid skirts, and boat shoes – basically all the classic pieces that give off major rich-kid vibes. You can never go wrong with a good pair of chinos and a crisp button-down shirt. And let’s not forget about those adorable little cardigans that are perfect for layering. Whether you’re going for a preppy schoolgirl look or a sophisticated gentleman vibe, there’s something for everyone in the preppy fashion world. Plus, it’s like dressing up without actually having to dress up, if you know what I mean.
