# Travel and holiday jokes — 2026-03-30

Hey fellow globetrotters! Let's spice up our travel adventures with some holiday-themed jokes that will have you rolling like a suitcase down a cobblestone street! 🌍✈️

1. Why did the scarecrow win an award for best travel companion?
Because he was outstanding in his field! 🌾

2. What do you call a snowman on vacation?

5. How does Santa take his vacations?
