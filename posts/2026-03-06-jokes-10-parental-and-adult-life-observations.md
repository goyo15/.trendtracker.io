# Parental and adult life observations — 2026-03-06

Hey fam, let’s talk about one of the oldest debates in the book – parental and adult life observations. 🙄 It’s like once you hit a certain age, you automatically forget what it’s like to be a teen. We get it, adulthood comes with responsibilities, but do adults have to be so...boring?

Parents always be like “We were young once too,” but do they really remember how it feels to be constantly stressed about school, friendships, and the future? And don’t even get me started on their obsession with technology – they act like we’re the ones glued to our phones all day!

But hey, let’s cut them some slack. They do have bills to pay and real-life stuff to deal with. Maybe we’ll understand their struggle when we’re in the same shoes one day. But until then, can they at least stop giving us the “Back in my day” lecture? 🙄 #TeenLife #ParentalObservations
