# Cottagecore - A trend focusing on a romanticized rural aesthetic, incorporating flowy dresses, floral patterns, and nature-inspired accessories. — 2026-04-27

So, if you’re into soft, dreamy aesthetics, this trend is definitely worth checking out. Get yourself some flowy dresses, cute floral patterns, and nature-inspired accessories to channel those cottagecore vibes.
