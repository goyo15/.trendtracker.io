# "Dry Scooping" fitness challenge — 2026-06-23

Hey fam, have y'all heard about this new fitness craze called "dry scooping"? Apparently, it's all the rage on TikTok, but let me tell you something – it's a disaster waiting to happen.

Basically, dry scooping involves dumping a scoop of pre-workout powder straight into your mouth without mixing it with water. Sounds wild, right? Well, here's the tea: it can be super dangerous. This challenge can lead to choking, dehydration, and even heart problems. And let's not forget about that powder getting stuck in your throat – not a cute look.

So, if you're thinking about trying dry scooping for clout, think again. Our health is the real flex, and risking it for a few likes just isn't worth it. Stick to drinking your pre-workout the normal way, stay hydrated, and remember – safety first, always. Stay savvy, squad! #StaySafeStaySavvy
