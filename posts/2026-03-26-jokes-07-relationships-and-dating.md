# Relationships and dating — 2026-03-26

Hey fam, let’s talk relationships and dating – the ultimate teen drama, am I right? It’s like a rollercoaster of emotions in the jungle of love. One minute you’re head over heels crushing on someone, and the next you’re questioning if they even know you exist. But hey, that’s part of the game. 

My advice? Stay true to yourself and don’t settle for anything less than butterflies in your stomach. If they don’t make you smile like a fool or stay up all night chatting, then they’re not worth your time. Be open, communicate, and don’t be afraid to set boundaries. And remember, it’s okay to be single and focus on yourself too.

So keep swiping, flirting, and maybe even falling in love – just make sure it’s worth the ride. And if it all goes south, well, at least you’ll have some good stories to share with your squad. Stay fierce, stay true, and happy dating, fam!✌🏼💕
