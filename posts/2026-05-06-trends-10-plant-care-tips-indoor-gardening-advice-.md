# Plant care tips: Indoor gardening advice and plant care guides. — 2026-05-06

Hey there, plant parents! Ready to take your indoor garden to the next level? Check out these must-know plant care tips to keep your leafy babies thriving! 

First off, light is key. Make sure to place your plants in spots where they can soak up that sweet, sweet sunshine. Different plants have different light requirements, so do your research and match them with the right location in your home.

Next up, water wisely. Overwatering is a major no-no, so let the top inch of soil dry out before you reach for that watering can. And don’t forget to give your plants a little love with some gentle misting to keep them feeling fresh and fabulous.

Lastly, keep an eye out for any signs of trouble. Wilted leaves, yellowing, or pests? Don’t panic, just do a little detective work to figure out what your plant needs.
