# Y2K fashion — 2026-06-07

Hey everyone, let’s talk about Y2K fashion–the trend that’s making a major comeback! From low-rise jeans to mini skirts and butterfly clips, the iconic style of the early 2000s is hotter than ever. 

Get ready to channel your inner Paris Hilton or Britney Spears with crop tops, platform sandals, and blingy accessories. Think Juicy Couture tracksuits, Von Dutch trucker hats, and anything covered in rhinestones.
