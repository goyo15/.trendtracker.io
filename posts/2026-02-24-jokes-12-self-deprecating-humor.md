# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-02-24

Hey, fam! Let’s talk about self-deprecating humor. It’s like that funny friend who’s always roasting themselves for a quick laugh. And yeah, it can be pretty hilarious, no cap. But here’s the tea – there’s a fine line between keeping it real and putting yourself down for clout.

Sure, poking fun at yourself can be a vibe sometimes, but constantly dissing yourself ain’t it, sis. You’re a boss babe with goals and dreams, so why sell yourself short for a couple of giggles? Remember, self-love is a whole mood. Own your imperfections, but don’t let them define you.

So, next time you’re about to drop a self-deprecating joke, ask yourself, “Is this bringing good vibes or just boosting my insecurities?” Stay woke, stay positive, and slay like the queen you are.
