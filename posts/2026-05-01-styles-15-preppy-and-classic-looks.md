# Preppy and classic looks — 2026-05-01

Hey, fashionistas! Let’s talk preppy and classic looks. There’s just something about rocking a crisp white button-down shirt or a perfectly tailored blazer that screams sophistication. 

For a preppy aesthetic, think pleated skirts, cable knit sweaters, and boat shoes. Don’t be afraid to mix in some pops of color or fun patterns to keep it fresh and modern. 

When going for a classic look, you can never go wrong with a little black dress, a well-fitted pair of jeans, or a timeless trench coat. These pieces exude timeless elegance and are versatile enough to be dressed up or down for any occasion.

So whether you’re feeling preppy or classic, remember to add your own unique twist to make the look your own. Stay stylish, babes! 💁🏽‍♀️👗👠 #FashionGoals #PreppyAndClassic
