# Relationships and Dating — 2026-04-28

Hey, teens! Let's talk about relationships and dating - the messy, complicated, rollercoaster ride that it is. First things first, don't feel pressured to date just because everyone else is. Take your time and focus on yourself first. 

When you do start dating, communication is key! Be open and honest with your partner, and always listen to what they have to say. Remember, it's a two-way street. Don't forget to set boundaries and stand up for yourself if something doesn't feel right.

And hey, it's okay to make mistakes. That's how we learn and grow. Just make sure you're treating yourself and others with respect. Oh, and please, please don't ghost people. It's not cool.

Ultimately, your worth isn't defined by being in a relationship. Love yourself first, and everything else will fall into place. Stay true to yourself, have fun, and remember, you're a boss whether you're single or taken. Peace out, teen squad!
