# Viral challenges and pranks on social media platforms — 2026-04-22

Hey there, teens! Let’s talk about viral challenges and pranks on social media. Sure, watching someone attempt a crazy challenge or prank can be entertaining, but here’s the tea – some of these trends can be straight-up dangerous or harmful. 

Before you hit that retweet or share button, consider: is this challenge safe and do I really want to support it? Remember, just because everyone else is doing it doesn’t mean you have to follow suit. Your safety and well-being should always come first.

So, next time you come across a viral challenge or prank, think before you engage. Is it worth risking your health or reputation for a fleeting moment of internet fame? Let’s use our influence on social media for good, not for likes or views. Stay smart, stay safe, and keep on slaying, fam! ✌️ #ThinkBeforeYouClick #StaySafeStaySlaying
