# POV (Point of View) Videos - Users create narrative-driven clips from different perspectives to engage viewers. — 2026-07-13

Hey fam, let’s talk about POV videos - the latest trend that’s taking social media by storm! 🌪️ POV stands for Point of View, and it’s all about creating narrative-driven clips from unique perspectives to draw viewers in and keep them hooked. It’s like putting them right in the shoes of the storyteller and giving them a front-row seat to the action. 🎥

It’s all about making viewers feel like they’re part of the story, and that’s why these videos are so engaging and addictive! 💥
