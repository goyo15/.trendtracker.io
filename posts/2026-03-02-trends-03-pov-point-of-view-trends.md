# POV (Point of View) Trends — 2026-03-02

Hey, trendsetters! Let’s talk about the hottest thing in the writing world right now: Point of View (POV) trends. From classics like first-person and third-person limited, to edgy new styles like second-person and multiple POV narratives, there’s a POV for every vibe.

First-person POV is like spilling the tea directly from the protagonist’s mouth, giving us all the juicy deets on their thoughts and feels. Third-person limited is like stalking the protagonist from afar, but getting up close and personal with their inner monologue.

Feeling bold? Jump into second-person POV, where the protagonist is YOU and the story unfolds as if YOU are the main character. And don’t sleep on multiple POV narratives, where different characters take turns telling their side of the story.
