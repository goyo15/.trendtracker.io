# Plant Parenting - highlighting plant care tips and plant tours — 2026-06-19

Hey fellow plant parents!🌿💚 Today, I’m sharing some pro tips to level up your plant parenting game and give your green babies some extra love: 

1. Sunlight is key! Different plants have different light requirements, so make sure to place them in the right spot to keep them thriving.

2. Water wisely! Overwatering can be just as harmful as underwatering, so learn your plant’s specific watering needs and stick to a schedule.

3. Feed ‘em right! Fertilize your plants during growing season to give them the nutrients they need to keep growing strong.
