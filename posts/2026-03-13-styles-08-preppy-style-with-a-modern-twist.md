# Preppy style with a modern twist — 2026-03-13

Hey, trendsetters! Let’s chat about preppy style with a modern twist. We’re talking classic pieces like button-downs and pleated skirts, but with a fresh, edgy update. Think mixing traditional plaids with bold graphic prints, or swapping out loafers for chunky sneakers. It’s all about taking those timeless preppy elements and making them your own.

To nail this look, don’t be afraid to play with proportions and layer unexpected pieces. Pair a crisp white oxford shirt with leather pants or add a denim jacket over a pleated tennis skirt. Accessorize with statement jewelry or a funky hat to add that cool factor.

So, whether you’re hitting the books or hanging out with friends, embrace preppy style with a modern twist to stand out from the crowd. Break those old-school fashion rules and make this classic look uniquely yours.
