# Relationship advice and tips, especially targeted towards Gen Z. — 2026-03-13

Hey fam! Let’s talk relationships, Gen Z style! 🙌 Whether you’re crushing hard or in a committed sitch, here are some tips to help ya out. First off, communication is key! In a world of DMs and texts, make sure to have those real convos face-to-face. Trust me, it makes a huge diff. 🗣️ Next, don’t forget about boundaries! It’s cool to have your own space and hobbies – don’t lose yourself in a relationship. Also, be supportive of each other’s goals and dreams 💫. And lastly, always keep it real! Authenticity is what sets us apart, so stay true to yourself no matter what. Remember, you’re a boss babe or a king, and deserve someone who vibes with your awesomeness.
