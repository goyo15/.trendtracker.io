# Preppy schoolgirl / schoolboy look — 2026-07-13

Hey there, fashionistas! Let’s talk about the timeless and oh-so-chic preppy schoolgirl/schoolboy look that never goes out of style. This iconic look is all about mixing classic pieces with a modern twist to create a sophisticated yet fun ensemble. Think plaid skirts, Oxford shirts, cable-knit sweaters, and loafers – all staples of the preppy wardrobe.

To rock the preppy schoolgirl/schoolboy look, mix and match your pieces to create a look that is uniquely you. Pair a pleated skirt with a crisp white button-down shirt and a cozy cardigan for a touch of preppy charm. Don’t forget to accessorize with a cute headband or a sleek tie for that final polished touch.

Whether you’re headed to classes, a coffee date, or a study session, the preppy schoolgirl/schoolboy look is sure to turn heads and make a statement. So grab your favorite pieces, mix them up, and let your preppy style shine!
