# Travel and vacation jokes — 2026-06-05

Hey, jet-setters! Let’s talk about travel and vacation jokes that will have you laughing all the way to your next destination. Whether you’re cruising the coast or sightseeing in the city, a good joke is always a great travel companion. Need a pick-me-up during a long layover? Try this out: Why did the math book look sad during its vacation? Because it had too many problems. And who can forget the classic traveler’s dilemma: Why don’t skeletons go on vacation? They don’t have the guts! So next time you’re roaming the globe, pack some puns in your carry-on and get ready to giggle your way through any travel hiccup.
