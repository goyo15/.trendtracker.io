# Annoying parents and family jokes — 2026-03-24

Ugh, don’t you just hate it when your parents and family keep cracking the same old jokes over and over again? Whether it’s your mom teasing you about your crush or your dad embarrassing you in front of your friends, it feels like they never run out of material. And don’t even get me started on the classic dad joke – eye roll central! But hey, maybe they think they’re being funny and relatable, right? Still, it’s like, come on guys, can we switch it up a bit? How about some new material, please? So here’s to hoping our parents and relatives start taking a crash course in comedy or at least try out some fresh jokes. Who knows, maybe one day we’ll actually laugh instead of cringe. Till then, let’s just roll our eyes and play along – it’s all part of the family comedy routine, after all.
