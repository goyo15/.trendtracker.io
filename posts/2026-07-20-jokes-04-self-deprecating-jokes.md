# Self-deprecating jokes — 2026-07-20

Hey, all you cool cats and kittens! Let’s chat about self-deprecating jokes. 👀 Sure, we all have those moments when we want to make fun of ourselves, but when does it cross the line from humor to harmful? 🤔

Self-deprecation can be a way to connect with others and show humility, but constantly putting yourself down can seriously harm your self-esteem. Remember, you’re awesome just the way you are! 💪 It’s all about balance, peeps. So, if you catch yourself using self-deprecating humor as a defense mechanism, maybe take a step back and channel that energy into building yourself up instead. ✨

Let’s keep the jokes light and the self-love strong! 💖 Life’s too short to be your own harshest critic. Stay fabulous, stay true to yourself! 💁‍♀️ #SelfLove #NoMoreSelfBurns
