# Fashion and beauty faux pas — 2026-04-04

Hey babes and dudes! Let’s talk about some major fashion and beauty faux pas that we need to leave behind in the dust, like, ASAP. First up, wearing socks with sandals - it’s a TOTAL no-go, unless you’re going for that quirky grandpa-chic look. Next, over-tweezed brows are so yesterday - let those babies grow in a bit for that natural, bushy look. And don’t even get me started on visible foundation lines - blend, blend, blend, people!

As for clothing, say goodbye to wearing leggings as pants - they’re meant for layering, not as a substitute for jeans. And oversized clothing can be cute, but drowning in fabric is never a good look. Lastly, mismatched foundation shades are a big beauty blunder - always test on your jawline to find your perfect match.

Let’s steer clear of these fashion and beauty disasters to keep slaying our style game! 💅🏼👗 #FashionFails #BeautyBlunders
