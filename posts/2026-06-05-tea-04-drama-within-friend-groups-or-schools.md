# Drama within friend groups or schools — 2026-06-05

Hey, party people! Let’s talk about drama within friend groups or schools, because let’s be real, it’s everywhere. One day you’re gossiping with your BFFs, and the next day you’re dealing with shady rumors and backstabbing drama. It’s like a rollercoaster ride, minus the fun.
