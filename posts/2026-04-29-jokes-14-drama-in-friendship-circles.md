# Drama in friendship circles — 2026-04-29

Hey, fam! Let’s spill the tea ☕️ on drama in friendship circles. We’ve all been there – the eye-rolls, the whispers, the backstabbing. Drama is like glitter, it gets everywhere, but it’s way less fun. Friendships are supposed to be a safe space, but sometimes they feel more like a battlefield. 

Remember, not every disagreement has to turn into a full-blown feud. Sometimes you just gotta let things go like last season’s trend. Communication is key, so speak up or forever hold your peace. And always remember to surround yourself with real ones who lift you up, not bring you down. 

Life’s too short to be caught up in pettiness. Stay messy, but in a cool way. Let’s keep our friendship circles drama-free and our vibes 💯. Peace out! 🔥 #FriendshipGoals #NoDramaZone
