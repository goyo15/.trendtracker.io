# Grunge revival: Drawing from the '90s grunge scene, includes flannel shirts, combat boots, and distressed denim. — 2026-05-25

Hey there, fellow fashion rebels! Are you ready to embrace the grunge revival trend that's taking over 2021 by storm? That's right, we're talking about channeling our inner '90s rockstars with flannel shirts, combat boots, and distressed denim. 

Grunge fashion is all about embracing the raw and edgy style that made bands like Nirvana and Pearl Jam iconic. It's all about mixing and matching different textures and styles to create a look that's effortlessly cool and badass. Throw on a plaid flannel shirt over a band tee, pair it with some ripped jeans or a denim skirt, and complete the look with a pair of chunky combat boots - and voila, you're ready to rock the grunge look like a pro.
