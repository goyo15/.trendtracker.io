# Annoying habits of parents and teachers — 2026-03-29

Hey there, fellow teens! Let’s talk about the annoying habits of parents and teachers that make us roll our eyes so hard they nearly get stuck in the back of our heads.

First up, parents who never stop nagging about chores and responsibilities. Like, yes, we get it, we’ll clean our room... eventually. And don’t even get me started on the classic “back in my day” lectures – thanks, but no thanks.

Now, onto teachers with their constant reminders about deadlines and homework. We have 5 other classes to worry about, Karen, cut us some slack! And why do they always seem to think they’re the only subject we have to study? Newsflash, we’ve got a lot on our plates.

But hey, let’s give them some credit – they mean well (most of the time). So next time they’re driving you up the wall, just take a deep breath and remember that at least they care... even if it’s a bit much sometimes. Stay strong, my friends! ✌️ #teenlife
