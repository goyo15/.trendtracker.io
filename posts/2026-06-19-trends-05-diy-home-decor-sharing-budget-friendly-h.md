# DIY Home Decor - sharing budget-friendly home decor ideas — 2026-06-19

Hey, squad! Let's talk about sprucing up our spaces on a budget with some awesome DIY home decor ideas! 🌟 From revamping old furniture with a fresh coat of paint to creating your own wall art with thrifted frames and prints, the possibilities are endless. 

One of my go-to budget-friendly decor hacks is hitting up local thrift stores and flea markets for unique finds to upcycle and personalize. You never know what gems you might uncover! Another fun idea is DIY-ing your own custom throw pillows by sewing or even using fabric glue to jazz up plain pillow covers.

Don't forget about the power of plants! Adding some greenery to your space can instantly elevate the vibe and bring a touch of nature indoors. Plus, plants are super affordable and easy to care for.

So, let's get our creative juices flowing and transform our spaces into cozy and stylish havens without breaking the bank! 🌿💫 #DIYHomeDecor #StayThrifty
