# Gen Z minimalism — 2026-03-09

Hey, Gen Z crew! Let’s talk about the hot trend sweeping our generation – Gen Z minimalism. We’re ditching the clutter and embracing simplicity in all aspects of our lives. From decluttering our physical spaces to simplifying our digital presence, we’re all about that minimalist vibe.
