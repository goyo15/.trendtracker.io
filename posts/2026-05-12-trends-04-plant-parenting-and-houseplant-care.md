# Plant parenting and houseplant care — 2026-05-12

Hey plant parents, if you’ve been trying to keep up with the houseplant trend but your green babies keep wilting on you, fear not – I've got your back. Plant parenting is a responsibility, but it doesn’t have to be a chore. First rule of thumb: don’t overwater! Your plants need love, not drowning. Second, give them some sun – they’re not vampires, after all. And third, show them a little attention every now and then – talk to them, play some music, whatever floats their foliage. Remember, keeping up with plant care isn’t just a millennial aesthetic, it’s a lifestyle. So grab a watering can, put on your gardening gloves, and show those leafy roommates some love.
