# Oversized and baggy silhouettes — 2026-05-19

Hey trendsetters! Let’s talk about oversized and baggy silhouettes - the ultimate comfy trend that’s taking over the fashion world right now. From slouchy hoodies to boyfriend jeans, rocking oversized pieces is all about making a statement while staying cozy AF. 

Forget about skin-tight clothes, because the baggier, the better! It’s all about that effortless, cool-girl vibe that screams “I woke up like this”. Plus, who doesn’t love a little extra room to move and groove?

But remember, there’s an art to pulling off this trend. Balance is key, so pair your oversized top with fitted bottoms or vice versa to keep your look on point. And don’t be afraid to play with proportions and layer up for a super stylish ensemble.
