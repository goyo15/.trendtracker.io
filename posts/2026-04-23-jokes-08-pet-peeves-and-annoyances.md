# Pet peeves and annoyances — 2026-04-23

Hey there, fellow teens! Let’s chat about pet peeves and annoyances, because let’s be real, we all have them. Whether it’s someone chewing loudly, slow walkers in the hallway, or when your phone dies at 1%, we all have those little things that drive us bonkers.

Don’t even get me started on when people leave unread notifications on their phone – like, just open it already! And what about when someone interrupts you while you’re talking? Rude much?

But hey, let’s not dwell on the negative vibes too long. Instead, let’s focus on the things that make us happy, like getting a text from your crush or finding money in your pocket that you didn’t know you had.

So, next time your pet peeves start to get to you, take a deep breath, count to ten, and maybe just maybe, try not to let them annoy you too much. Life’s too short to sweat the small stuff, am I right? Peace out, squad!
