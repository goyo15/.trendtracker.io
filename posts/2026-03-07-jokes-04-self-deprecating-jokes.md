# Self-deprecating jokes — 2026-03-07

Hey, fam! Let’s talk about self-deprecating jokes – you know, those witty quips we make about ourselves to get a quick laugh. It can be tempting to use self-deprecation as a way to fit in or seem more relatable, but let’s keep it real – constantly putting ourselves down isn’t a good look. It’s like wearing a cute outfit but then telling everyone it’s ugly – not a vibe!

Sure, a funny self-roast here and there can be fun, but when it becomes your go-to move, it can start affecting your self-esteem. Remember, you’re the main character of your own story, so why not hype yourself up instead of tearing yourself down?

So, drop the self-deprecating jokes and start embracing your uniqueness. You’re amazing, and you deserve to shine bright like the queen/king you are! Keep it real and keep it positive, fam. Peace out! ✌️ #PositiveVibesOnly
