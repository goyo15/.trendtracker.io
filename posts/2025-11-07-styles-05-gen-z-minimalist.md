# Gen-Z Minimalist — 2025-11-07

Hey, fellow teens! Let's talk about Gen-Z Minimalists - the absolute game-changers of our generation! These trendsetters are all about quality over quantity, ditching clutter for clean and chic spaces. From capsule wardrobes to decluttered rooms, they're all about that Marie Kondo vibe - sparking joy and getting rid of anything that doesn't serve a purpose. 

But it's not just about stuff - it's a whole lifestyle. Gen-Z Minimalists are all about sustainability and conscious consumerism. They opt for reusable items, shop second-hand, and support ethical brands. Saving the planet while looking super stylish? Yes, please!
