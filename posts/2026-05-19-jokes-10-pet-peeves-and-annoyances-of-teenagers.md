# Pet peeves and annoyances of teenagers — 2026-05-19

Ugh, let’s talk about pet peeves and annoyances that teenagers deal with on the daily. First up, slow walkers. Like, why are you strolling at a glacial pace when I’m walking behind you at warp speed? Seriously, pick up the pace! And don’t even get me started on people who chew with their mouths open. It’s like, can you not? The sound alone is enough to drive me up the wall.

Also, can we talk about fake friends? You know, the ones who act all nice to your face but then throw shade behind your back. Like, we see right through you, sweetie. And don’t even think about asking to copy my homework last minute - I’m not your personal Google Drive.

Teenagers have a lot on their plates as it is, so let’s try to cut each other some slack and maybe, just maybe, we can all coexist without driving each other insane. Deal? Deal.
