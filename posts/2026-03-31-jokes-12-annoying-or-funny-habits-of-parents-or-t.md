# Annoying or funny habits of parents or teachers — 2026-03-31

Hey there, fam! Let's talk about the most annoying (yet sometimes funny) habits of parents and teachers that make us roll our eyes so hard they almost get stuck back there.

Parents, oh parents. Why do they always have to use "back in my day" as an excuse for everything? Like, cool, mom and dad, we get it, you had to walk to school uphill both ways in the snow. But let's be real, as annoying as their habits may be, they do make us laugh sometimes. Like when your dad tries to dab or your teacher accidentally calls your classmate by your name. It's all in good fun, right?
