# Self-care routines and wellness trends — 2026-05-16

Hey there, fellow teens! Let's talk self-care and wellness trends. Self-care isn't just about bubble baths and face masks (although those are nice too). It's about taking care of both your physical and mental well-being. 

One wellness trend that's been gaining popularity is meditation and mindfulness. Taking a few minutes each day to center yourself and focus on the present moment can do wonders for reducing stress and anxiety. Another trend is journaling - writing down your thoughts can be a great way to process emotions and reflect on your day.

And let's not forget about exercise! Whether it's dancing, yoga, or simply going for a walk, moving your body is key to feeling good inside and out.

Remember, self-care looks different for everyone, so find what works best for you and make it a priority. Your mental and physical health are worth it! Stay happy and healthy, my friends. ✌️ #SelfCareSunday
