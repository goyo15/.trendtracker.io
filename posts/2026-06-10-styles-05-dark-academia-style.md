# Dark academia style: — 2026-06-10

Hey fam, let's talk about dark academia style – the ultimate vibe for those who love a little mystery and sophistication in their fashion game. Think old-school vibes mixed with a touch of edginess. We’re talking about blazers, turtlenecks, pleated skirts, and oxford shoes – all in a moody color palette of blacks, browns, and deep greens.

To nail this look, add some vintage accessories like a classic watch or a statement pair of glasses. Embrace your inner bookworm with a stack of old books as a chic prop. And don’t forget to top it off with a bold lip or a messy bun for that effortlessly cool aesthetic.

Dark academia style isn’t just about the clothes – it’s a whole vibe. Get inspired by classic literature, art, and architecture. So, grab your favorite leather-bound journal and let’s channel our inner intellectual badasses in style. It’s time to slay, my fellow academics! ✨📚🖤
