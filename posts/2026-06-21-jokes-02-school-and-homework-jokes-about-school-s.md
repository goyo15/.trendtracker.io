# School and Homework - jokes about school subjects, teachers, and student life — 2026-06-21

Hey fellow teens! Let’s talk about the daily grind of school and endless homework. Math problems? More like math problems that seem to multiply like rabbits. English essays? Yeah, can’t we just summarize the whole book in a tweet instead? And don’t even get me started on history class - because who doesn’t love memorizing dates of events that seem to have no relevance to our lives whatsoever?

Oh, and the teachers - we love ‘em, right? Especially when they decide to give us surprise quizzes on a Monday morning when we’re still half-asleep. Shoutout to the teachers who say they understand teenage struggles, but then assign a mountain of homework due the next day. 

But despite all the eye-rolling and sighing, we’ve gotta admit, school life does come with its fair share of hilarious moments. Like that one classmate who always manages to fall asleep during the most boring lectures, or the iconic cafeteria food that’s a mystery to us all. So, let’s embrace the chaos and power through, one spilt coffee on our textbooks at a time. Stay snarky, my friends!
