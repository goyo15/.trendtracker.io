# Oversized everything - Baggy tops, wide-leg pants, and oversized outerwear for a relaxed streetwear look. — 2025-11-21

Hey, trendsetters! Let’s talk about the ultimate streetwear vibe that’s taking the fashion world by storm - oversized EVERYTHING! Baggy tops, wide-leg pants, and oversized outerwear are the keys to achieving that effortlessly cool and laid-back style that screams “I woke up like this”.

Ditch the tight clothes and embrace the comfort and chicness of oversized pieces.
