# Book Recommendations - Sharing favorite book titles and reviews — 2026-06-07

Hey bookworms! 📚 Looking for your next page-turner? Let’s swap some book recommendations and reviews! Here are a few of my current faves:

1. “The Hate U Give” by Angie Thomas - It’s a powerful and eye-opening read that tackles important issues like racism and police brutality.

2. “Simon vs. the Homo Sapiens Agenda” by Becky Albertalli - This one will make you laugh, cry, and feel all the feels. Plus, the movie adaptation (“Love, Simon”) is just as amazing.

3. “The Cruel Prince” by Holly Black - If you’re into dark fantasy and faeries, this series will sweep you off your feet with its twists and turns.
