Yo, skater style is where it's at, my dudes! 🤙 Baggy pants? Check. Graphic tees? Double check. Beanies and Vans sneakers? Oh yeah, we're talking the full skater package here. It's all about that laid-back, don't-care attitude that comes straight from the skateboarding scene.
