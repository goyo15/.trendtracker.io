# Gen Z preppy: Updating classic preppy looks with a modern twist such as pleated skirts, collared shirts, and loafers. — 2026-01-13

Hey fellow Gen Z fashionistas, let's talk about giving preppy style a major upgrade! We all know the classics - pleated skirts, collared shirts, and loafers - but who says we can't put our own spin on it? 

Mixing and matching classic preppy pieces with contemporary trends is the key to creating a fresh and stylish look. Pair a pleated skirt with a graphic tee for some edge, or layer a cropped sweater over a collared shirt for a cozy-chic vibe. And those loafers? Rock them with some colorful socks for a fun pop of personality.

Don't be afraid to experiment and make the preppy style your own. Add some statement accessories, play with different textures, or even throw in some bold patterns to take your look to the next level.
