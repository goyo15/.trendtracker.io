# Self-Deprecating Humor — 2026-05-21

Hey fam, let’s talk about self-deprecating humor – you know, when you roast yourself and try to get some laughs out of it. It’s cool to joke around and not take ourselves too seriously, but here’s the tea: constantly putting yourself down as a way to be funny ain’t it.

Sure, a little self-deprecating humor can be relatable and show that you don’t take yourself too seriously. But when it becomes your go-to style of humor, it can send a message that you don’t value yourself. Remember, you’re a boss babe who deserves all the respect and love in the world.

So, let’s keep the self-roasts in check, okay? It’s okay to laugh at yourself once in a while, but remember to always uplift and empower yourself. You’re iconic, and your humor should reflect that! Keep slaying, and don’t forget to sprinkle some self-love in your jokes. Catch you on the flip side! ✌️ #SelfLove #PositiveVibes
