# Preppy/Collegiate - Classic and polished look with button-down shirts, blazers, and loafers. — 2026-05-08

Hey there, fashionistas! Let's talk about the timeless preppy/collegiate style that’s been making a major comeback lately. Think: classic and polished vibes with a nod to Ivy League campuses.

Button-down shirts, blazers, and loafers are the holy trinity of this style, giving off major Gossip Girl and Clueless vibes. It’s all about looking put-together and sophisticated, but with a dash of youthful charm.

Mix and match your favorite pieces to create a look that's uniquely you. Pair a crisp white button-down with high-waisted jeans and loafers for a chic, casual vibe. Or go all out with a plaid blazer, pleated skirt, and oxford shoes for a more formal twist.

Don't be afraid to add your own flair to the preppy look - whether it's funky accessories or a bold pop of color. So, channel your inner Blair Waldorf and embrace the preppy/collegiate style with confidence! 👔👠
