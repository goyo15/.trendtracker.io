# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-04-23

Hey, fellow teens! Let’s chat about self-deprecating humor – you know, when you crack jokes about yourself to get some laughs. It can be hilarious and relatable, but here’s the thing: we need to know where to draw the line.

Self-deprecation is all fun and games until it starts affecting your self-esteem. Sure, poking fun at our own quirks can be a mood booster, but constantly putting ourselves down ain’t it. We’re all awesome in our own ways, so why make ourselves the punchline all the time?

Let’s practice self-love and remember that we’re more than just our flaws. It’s rad to have a good sense of humor, but let’s make sure it’s not at the expense of our self-worth. Embrace your uniqueness, rock your strengths, and leave the self-deprecation behind. You’re way too cool for that. Stay fierce, my friends! ✌️ #LoveYourself #PositiveVibes
