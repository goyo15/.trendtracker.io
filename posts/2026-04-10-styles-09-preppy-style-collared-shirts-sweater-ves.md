# Preppy style - Collared shirts, sweater vests, pleated skirts — 2026-04-10

Hey there, fellow fashionistas! Let’s talk about the preppy style trend making a major comeback - collared shirts, sweater vests, and pleated skirts. 

These classic pieces are giving off major Ivy League vibes and adding a touch of sophistication to our wardrobes. Collared shirts are the perfect mix of chic and casual, while sweater vests are super versatile and can be styled in countless ways. And let’s not forget about pleated skirts - they’re a total must-have for that preppy schoolgirl look.

Mix and match these pieces to create your own unique preppy style that is sure to turn heads. Whether you’re channeling Clueless vibes or a modern preppy twist, these wardrobe staples are here to elevate your fashion game. So grab your favorite collared shirt, throw on a cute sweater vest, and twirl around in a pleated skirt - you’ll be serving some serious preppy vibes in no time! #PrepsterPerfection
