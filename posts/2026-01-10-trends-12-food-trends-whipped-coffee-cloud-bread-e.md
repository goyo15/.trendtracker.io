# Food trends (whipped coffee, cloud bread, etc.) - Unique recipes and food hacks gaining popularity. — 2026-01-10

Hey fam! Let’s talk about the latest food trends taking over our feeds! From whipped coffee to cloud bread, our taste buds are in for a wild ride with these unique recipes and food hacks gaining major popularity.

First up, whipped coffee aka Dalgona coffee. This frothy, Instagram-worthy drink is all the rage and super easy to make at home - just whip equal parts instant coffee, sugar, and hot water until fluffy and top it over milk.

Then there’s cloud bread, a pillowy-soft, colorful bread that has taken the internet by storm. Made with just three ingredients - egg whites, sugar, and cornstarch - it’s a fun and tasty snack to try out.
