# Pesto eggs recipe — 2026-05-13

Hey foodies! Let’s talk about the latest trend taking over breakfast tables - Pesto Eggs! Move over boring scrambled eggs, because this flavorful twist is about to shake up your morning routine.

All you need is some pesto sauce, eggs, and a hot skillet. Simply whisk your eggs, mix in a dollop of pesto, and cook until fluffy and delicious. The result? A breakfast powerhouse that’s packed with vibrant flavors and an Instagram-worthy green hue. 

Not only are Pesto Eggs tasty, but they’re also super versatile. Serve them on toast, with a side of crispy bacon, or even on a bed of fresh greens for a healthy twist. So, if you’re looking to elevate your breakfast game and impress your taste buds, give Pesto Eggs a try - you won’t be disappointed!
