# Food and snacks humor (especially fast food chains) — 2026-03-21

What's the deal with fast food chains and their over-the-top marketing tactics? I mean, do they really expect us to believe that their burgers are made with "all-natural, farm-fresh ingredients"? Please, we all know it's more like "questionable mystery meat slathered in a gallon of mayo."

And don't even get me started on the sizes they offer. "Would you like a small, medium, large, extra-large, or heart-attack-inducing mega-gargantuan size?" Like are we ordering food or competing in a weightlifting competition?

But hey, you gotta admit, there's something oddly comforting about diving into a greasy bag of fries or sipping on a super-sized soda. It's like our guilty pleasure, you know? And let's face it, nothing beats the feeling of snagging a free extra dipping sauce or scoring that last chicken nugget.

So here's to the quirky humor and absurdity of fast food chains – may they never change, and may our cholesterol levels forgive us.
