# School and homework — 2026-06-29

Hey, fellow students! Let’s talk about the ultimate buzzkill - school and homework. I mean, we all know they’re necessary evils, but does that make them any less annoying? Nope.

But hey, maybe there’s a silver lining. School is where we see our friends every day, learn new things (even if we don’t always care), and hey, at least it’s a step closer to graduation, right?

So, let’s power through this together. Take breaks, stay organized, and remember, we’re all in this struggle-fest called school and homework together.
