# Self-deprecating humor — 2026-07-04

Hey guys, let’s talk about self-deprecating humor – the good, the bad, and the cringeworthy. Yeah, it can be pretty hilarious to poke fun at ourselves and our flaws, but when is it going too far? It’s all fun and games until we start genuinely believing the negative things we say about ourselves.

Sure, a little self-deprecation can make you seem more relatable and approachable, but constantly putting yourself down can damage your self-esteem in the long run. It’s like trying to roast yourself before anyone else can, but sometimes those jokes hit a little too close to home.

So, let’s keep the self-deprecating humor in check. It’s cool to laugh at yourself from time to time, just remember that you’re awesome and deserve to be your own biggest fan. Keep the jokes light, keep them funny, and above all, keep that self-love strong. Stay snappy, stay sassy!✌️ #SelfLove #LaughNotLose
