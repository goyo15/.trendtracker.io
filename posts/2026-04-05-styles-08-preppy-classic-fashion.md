# Preppy/classic fashion — 2026-04-05

Hey fashionistas! Let's talk preppy/classic style - the OG of fashion trends! 🌟 Think timeless pieces like polo shirts, plaid skirts, and boat shoes. It's all about that clean, polished look that never goes out of style. 

Don't be fooled, preppy doesn't mean boring! You can totally rock this look with a modern twist. Pair a classic blazer with ripped jeans, or mix a button-down shirt with edgy combat boots. 

Accessorize with a statement watch, a cute headband, or some pearl earrings for that extra touch of sophistication. And don't forget a cute tote bag or a classic backpack to complete the look. 

Preppy/classic fashion is all about looking put-together and confident. So grab your favorite pieces, mix and match, and let your style shine! 💫 #preppyvibes #classyandfabulous
