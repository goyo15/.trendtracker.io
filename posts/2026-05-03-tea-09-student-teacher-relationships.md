# Student-teacher relationships — 2026-05-03

Hey my fellow teens! Let’s talk about student-teacher relationships. Look, we spend a lot of time with our teachers, so it’s important to try and have a positive connection with them. Not saying we have to be besties, but a little mutual respect can go a long way. 

Don’t be afraid to ask questions, seek advice, or just have a chat with your teachers. They’re there to help us succeed, so let’s take advantage of that. And hey, let’s also remember that teachers are human too, they have bad days and good days just like we do.

Of course, boundaries are important. Let’s keep it professional and respectful at all times. No need to blur the lines between teacher and friend. So, let’s strive for a healthy student-teacher relationship – one that’s built on trust, communication, and mutual understanding. Let’s make high school a positive experience for everyone! ✌️ #StudentTeacherVibes
