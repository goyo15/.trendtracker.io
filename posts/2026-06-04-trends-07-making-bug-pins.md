# Making Bug Pins — 2026-06-04

Hey, my fellow DIY enthusiasts! Today, I wanted to share one of my latest crafting obsessions: making bug pins! 🐞🦋 Whether you’re into creepy crawlies or just looking for a fun and unique accessory, bug pins are the way to go.

All you need are some plain pins, small plastic bug toys, and a hot glue gun. Simply glue the bugs onto the pins, let them dry, and voilà – you’ve got yourself a cute and quirky accessory to add some pizzazz to your outfit!
