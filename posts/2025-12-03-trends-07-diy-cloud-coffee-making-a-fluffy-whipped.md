# "DIY Cloud Coffee" - Making a fluffy whipped coffee drink at home, also known as Dalgona Coffee. — 2025-12-03

Hey, trendy teens! Let’s chat about the latest coffee craze taking over social media: DIY Cloud Coffee, aka Dalgona Coffee. This fluffy whipped coffee drink has been blowing up our feeds, and it’s time to give it a try at home. All you need is instant coffee, sugar, hot water, and milk.

Simply whisk together equal parts of instant coffee, sugar, and hot water until it becomes a light and airy whipped texture. Pour it over a glass of milk, and voila – you’ve got yourself a creamy and delicious Cloud Coffee to enjoy!
