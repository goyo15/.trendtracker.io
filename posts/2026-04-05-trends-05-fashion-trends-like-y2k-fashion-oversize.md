# Fashion trends, like Y2K fashion, oversized blazers, and cottagecore fashion — 2026-04-05

Hey there, fashionistas! Let’s talk about the hottest trends taking the fashion world by storm right now. From Y2K vibes to oversized blazers and cottagecore cuteness, it’s all about embracing unique styles and making a statement.

And let’s not forget about cottagecore fashion, bringing us dreamy prairie dresses, floral patterns, and cozy knits that transport us to a whimsical countryside escape. Pair a flowy dress with some ankle boots for a look that’s both romantic and trendy.
