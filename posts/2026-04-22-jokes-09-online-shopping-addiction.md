# Online shopping addiction — 2026-04-22

Hey guys, let’s talk about online shopping addiction - the struggle is real, am I right? 🛍💸 It starts with innocent browsing, then BAM, your cart is stacked with stuff you probably don’t need but gotta have. Every parcel delivery feels like Christmas morning, until you check your bank account and realize you’re broke. 😬 So, how do we break this cycle of clicking ‘Add to Cart’ like it’s our job? Start by setting a budget and sticking to it. Delete those shopping apps from your phone, and resist the urge to browse when you’re bored. Find alternative ways to cope with stress or boredom, like going for a walk or calling a friend. Remember, you are more than the things you buy online! Let’s kick that shopping addiction to the curb and start stacking up those savings instead. You got this! 💪🛒 #BudgetQueen #RetailTherapyNoMore
