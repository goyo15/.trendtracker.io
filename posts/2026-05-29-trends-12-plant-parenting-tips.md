# Plant parenting tips — 2026-05-29

Hey plant parents! 🌿💚 Let’s talk about keeping those leafy babies thriving and Instagram-worthy! Here are some snappy plant parenting tips:

1. LIGHT: Different plants have different light needs. Research your plant friends and find the perfect spot for them to soak up those rays.

2. WATER: Don’t drown your plants! Stick your finger in the soil to check if they need a drink. Remember, it’s better to underwater than overwater.

3. LOVE: Show your plants some love! Talk to them, play music, or even give them a little spin every now and then. They’ll appreciate the attention.

4. REPOTTING: Give your plants room to grow by repotting them when they outgrow their current home. Upgrade their pot and soil to keep them happy.
