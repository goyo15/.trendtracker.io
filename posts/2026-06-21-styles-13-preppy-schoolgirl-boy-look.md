# Preppy schoolgirl/boy look — 2026-06-21

Hey fam! Let’s talk about the preppy schoolgirl/boy look that’s making a major comeback this season! 📚👔 Whether you’re rocking a plaid skirt or a crisp button-down shirt, this style is all about looking polished and put-together. Think Gossip Girl vibes with a modern twist! 🔥

For the girls, pair a pleated skirt with a fitted sweater for a classic prepster look. And don’t forget the knee-high socks and loafers for that extra preppy touch! For the guys, try layering a sweater over a collared shirt and finish off the look with tailored chinos and loafers.

Embrace the varsity jackets, argyle prints, and statement accessories to really take your preppy game to the next level. It’s all about mixing sophistication with a dash of playfulness! 🎓💅 Stay stylish, babes! #PreppyStyle #BackToSchoolChic
