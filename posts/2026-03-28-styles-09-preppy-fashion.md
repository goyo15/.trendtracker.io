# Preppy fashion — 2026-03-28

Hey fashionistas! Let’s talk about preppy style - it's all about that classic, clean look that's as sharp as a freshly-sharpened pencil. Think collared shirts, pleated skirts, and boat shoes that scream "I'm headed to the country club, but I'm too cool for school." 

Preppy fashion isn’t just for the East Coast elites anymore. It’s all about mixing and matching to make it your own. Pair a pastel cardigan with ripped jeans or throw on some pearls with your graphic tee for a quirky twist. 

Rocking preppy style isn’t about following the rules - it’s about breaking them in your own stylish way. So grab your favorite blazer, pop that collar, and show the world that you can be classy AND sassy. Prep it up, babes! 🎀 #PreppyPerfection
